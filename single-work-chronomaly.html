Chronomaly

Chronomaly throws players into a dark universe where science meets supernatural horror. In this 4-player cooperative experience, you and your fellow astronaut scientists breach the boundaries of time and space through mysterious portals, venturing into nightmarish realms in search of valuable specimens. Each expedition takes your team to different horror-filled environments - from fog-shrouded Victorian mansions to derelict space stations frozen in time. As you collect specimens for your research, you'll discover that these temporal anomalies have attracted entities beyond human comprehension. Your scientific endeavor quickly becomes a desperate fight for survival against cosmic horrors that shouldn't exist.
